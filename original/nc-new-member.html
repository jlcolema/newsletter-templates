I wanted to welcome you to our church as an official member! We are excited to have you as part of our family and part of the body of Christ!

Because we want to help you connect and feel at home, we designed a New Member page. Click on the links to take the next steps in getting involved.

Our mission statement is "to build a community where people can Come as they are, Meet Christ, Grow in their faith, and find a place to Serve." You've already experienced the first two, and we think the best way to experience growth is to get connected with a small group (if you haven't already). On the New Member page you'll find a link to Join a Life Group that will help us connect you with a group that best fits your needs. You can also find more information about groups on our Life Groups page.

Finding a place to Serve is another important step. Online, you can see all the different areas of ministry --everything from helping with our children's or student ministry to serving as a greeter or parking team member. Under our link "Find a Place to Serve" on the New Member page you can submit your form online or print and turn it in to the church office, so we can get you connected to serve.

Generosity means giving our time, service, spiritual gifts, and money to God. So, we also ask that you join us in giving to God financially by filling out the online giving form under the Give Now link on the New Member page. Our prayer for you is that you will learn to give in proportion to His blessings in your life.

We offer electronic giving, online giving or numbered envelopes if you so desire. How you choose to give is not important. Our hope is that it will be a means of growing in faith and commitment. If you would like to learn more about why giving to the Church is important, an excellent article concerning tithing is also linked on the New Member page. If you have requested tithe envelopes, please note that due to our processing procedure, it may be two months before your first ones arrive.

Once again, welcome! We look forward to this wonderful faith adventure together! We also want to be here for you. When needs, trials or concerns arise in your life, let us know about them so our pastors and staff can serve you. You are part of our wonderful family!
