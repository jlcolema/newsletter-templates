We want to be big enough to meet the needs of the people and yet small enough to care about everyone. We have many options to help everyone find smaller, caring groups where they can get to know each other better and do life together.

The best place to start is Discover Crosspoint. The next Discover Crosspoint will be held on Sunday, January 10, from 9:30 to 10:45 a.m. in portable 1. If you have children, they may attend or may go to their normal room in the Kids Building.

There are also numerous activities for children and youth as well. On Thursdays we offer Celebrate Recovery, a program dealing with hurts, habits and hangups.

On Sunday, in addition to our worship services, we offer various children's Bible study classes and Kids Worship. We also have an excellent nursery at all services for children from birth through 3 years old in which they teach a Christian curriculum to instill a Godly foundation at this very young age.
