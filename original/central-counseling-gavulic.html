COUNSELING CENTER RIBBON CUTTING

July 10, 2019

Dear Friends,

We are excited to announce that the Crestview location of the Crosspoint Counseling Center will open Monday, July 15! I invite you to join us for an official ribbon cutting Sunday, July 21 at 2:30 p.m. on our North Crestview Campus located at 6268 Old Bethel Road. Tours of the center and a chance to meet some of our counseling staff will follow from 2:45 until 4:00 p.m. There will be refreshments in the lobby of the North Crestview Church from 2-4 pm..

Our Counseling Center’s team of professional counselors offer counseling for children, adolescents, individuals and couples. We accept Tri-Care East, Aetna, Cigna and Military One Source. For more information, or to schedule an appointment, call (850) 279-4576.

See you there!
