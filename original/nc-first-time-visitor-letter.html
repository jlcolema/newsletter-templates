Thank you for joining us for worship.  It was great to have you as our guest.  I hope to have the opportunity to personally get to know you in the coming weeks and months. 

We are really excited about what God is doing here at Crosspoint and hope you’ll find a place to get connected.  We work hard at making our church a welcoming place where everyone feels at home.  We want to be big enough to meet the needs of the people and yet small enough to care about everyone.  We have many options to help everyone find smaller, caring groups where they can get to know each other better and do life together.  Some of those opportunities include connecting in Life Groups and our “Celebrate Recovery” program (which focuses on helping people through life’s “hurts, habits, and hang-ups”).  

We also have Discover Crosspoint held monthly where we explain about what our church is all about and provides more opportunities to meet our staff and key volunteer leaders. Our next Discover Crosspoint will be held on Sunday, May 16, from 9:15—10:45 a.m. Snacks and childcare are provided.  

In addition to our Sunday morning worship service, we offer an amazing Crosspoint Kids program.  Our elementary aged children and preschoolers engage in a dynamic time of music, storytelling, and small group time.  We also have an excellent nursery for children from birth through 3 years old to begin connecting these children to Christ in ways that they understand and can grow in His love.   On Sunday nights we also offer a vibrant student ministry for middle school and high school students.  They meet weekly from 6:00-7:30 p.m.
