THANK YOU FOR YOUR GENEROSITY

January 28, 2016

Dear Friends,

Our Finance Team let me know you have recently begun giving to God through your tithe here at Crosspoint. Thank you so much! We are always thrilled when someone does this. It is a sign that you really love God, you love the church, and you love what we are doing here. I promise you we do not take that lightly! We will steward your gift well toward impacting the work of Christ through our church.

I want to share a passage with you, which is a good reminder and inspiration to why and how God calls us to give.

"Do not store up for yourselves treasures on earth, where moth and rust destroy, and where thieves break in and steal. But store up for yourselves treasures in heaven, where moth and rust do not destroy, and where thieves do not break in and steal. For where your treasure is, there your heart will be also." Matthew 6:19-21

In giving you are honoring what Jesus calls us to do, and for this we are very thankful.

Thank you for being God's financial partner here at Crosspoint.

See you Sunday,
