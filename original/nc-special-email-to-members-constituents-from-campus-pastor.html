21 DAYS OF PRAYER AND FASTING

Dear Church,

This week, our staff and prayer team have been made aware of lots of illnesses, injury, and difficult leadership changes around our campuses and the churches in our area. We have spent some extra time praying for these things and wanted to share this need with all of you!

Any of these events by themselves could be a coincidence, but when considered collectively there is an obvious trend. With this in mind, I'd love to invite our entire congregation to be in prayer for the physical, emotional, and spiritual health of the "Body of Christ" in our community (including our own campus).

From October 15 to November 4 we will be engaging in "21 Days of Prayer and Fasting," and we welcome anyone to join us. Fasting generally means abstaining from food for a season to remember that Christ is our source of strength, hope, and comfort. While we will not be fasting from food that entire time, we encourage people to fast on and off throughout that time, and we also encourage people to dis-engage from the other distractions that we look to for comfort and strength, and use that time to engage in prayer instead. If you're interested in receiving more information and a daily prayer focus during that time, please email Jeremy Thiess to sign up (jthiess@crosspoint.church).

Thanks for being ambitious to connect people to Christ not just here at Crosspoint, North Crestview, but in any way possible throughout Northwest Florida!

See you Sunday,
