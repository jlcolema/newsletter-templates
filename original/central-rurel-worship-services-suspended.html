WORSHIP SERVICES SUSPENDED

March 13, 2020

Dear Members and Friends of Crosspoint,

As you are well aware, the coronavirus has been upgraded to a global pandemic. The disease continues to spread while fear and uncertainty have taken hold of the world. Crises like this are an opportunity for the Body of Christ to demonstrate the calm and hope only God can provide, in our otherwise fear-driven world.

Although we've had no reports of anyone associated with Crosspoint being infected with COVID-19, there have been people infected within our community. Therefore, to help our community reduce the risk of spreading the virus, and in light of recommendations by Florida Governor Ron DeSantis to restrict large gatherings, Crosspoint is suspending its weekend worship services at its physical campuses, as well as all Sunday activities for the next two weekends (March 14/15 and March 21/22).

In addition, all church-related activities (meetings, life groups, ministries Supper-on-Saturday, etc.) are suspended during the week of March 15-22. The Bargain Box will close during this time, too.

Church offices will remain open and staff members will be available during normal office hours.

We will continue to offer online worship services on Sundays through our online campus, so please join us online at 9:30 and 11:00 a.m.: https://live.crosspoint.church

And as always, recordings of previous messages are available here: https://crosspoint.church/messages

Though our on-campus activities will be curtailed, Crosspoint is still active in its other ministries. Please continue your regular financial support by giving online at https://crosspoint.church/givenow or with the Crosspoint app. Thank you so much for your financial faithfulness during this time. We will weather this together spiritually and financially.

In the days ahead, we will continue to evaluate the Coronavirus situation and respond as necessary. Please continue to monitor our website and social media sites for the latest Crosspoint news.

Crosspoint will continue to be here for our members and its community. We will get through this crisis together with the help of God by offering the healing ministry of Jesus to our community. If you feel the need to speak with a pastor or other staff member, please call our main phone number, (850) 678-4411.
