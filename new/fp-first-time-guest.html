Thank you for joining us for worship at Crosspoint, Freeport Campus! It was great to have you as our Guest. I hope to have the opportunity to personally get to know you in the coming weeks and months. 


We are really excited about what God is doing here at Crosspoint and hope you’ll find a place to get connected. We work hard at making our church a welcoming place where everyone feels at home!


Our campus strives to be big enough to meet the needs of our community, and yet small enough to care about everyone. We have many options to help everyone find smaller, caring groups where they can get to know each other better and do life together. Some of those opportunities include connecting with in-person and virtual Life Groups available at various times throughout the week.  


We also have Discover Crosspoint held monthly where we explain about what our church is all about and provides more opportunities to meet our staff and key volunteer leaders. Our next Discover Crosspoint will be held on Sunday,  June 10th, immediately following our Sunday service. Simply email freeport@crosspoint.church or text "DiscoverCP" to 474747 if you’d like to sign up or receive more information.  


In addition to our Sunday morning worship service, we offer an amazing Crosspoint Kids program. Our elementary aged children and preschoolers engage in a dynamic time of music, storytelling, and small group time.  We also have an excellent nursery for children from birth through 3 years old to begin connecting these children to Christ in ways that they understand and can grow in His love.


Please feel free to call the church for more information/assistance, or email me at sbrown@crosspoint.church with any questions about our Freeport or Online campuses.
