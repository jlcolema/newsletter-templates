Thank you for signing up for the Discover Crosspoint, which will be held this coming Sunday, July 8. 

The purpose of Discover Crosspoint is to introduce our vision, mission and values, but also to help you take next steps in your faith to grow through Life Groups and Serving. Discover Crosspoint is a requirement for those wishing to join in membership, however, membership is completely optional -- no pressure at all!

To get started:

  • Please save this link: https://crosspoint.church/discover/virtual -- password: virtual.
  • It would be helpful if you would download and take a look at the 10 page booklet so you can familiarize yourself with some of the material! We will have physical copies on hand for the event.
  • We will ask you to complete a "Household Roll" please provide your names, contact information, birth dates, etc.
  • There are also convenient links for you to sign up for a Life Group, or find your place to serve.
  • All of these things will help you get better connected at Crosspoint!

We look forward to meeting together Sunday!
