YEAR-END GIVING OPPORTUNITIES

December 26, 2017

Dear Friends,

We hope you have had a wonderful Christmas celebrating our Lord and Savior.

As we close out 2017 and reflect on all the ways God has blessed us, please consider giving an end of the year gift and help further our vision to build a community where people can come as they are, meet Christ, grow in their faith, and find a place to serve.

Because of the generosity of all the Crosspoint family, we continue to see lives changed in our kids, students and adult ministries as people get connected to Christ. We were able to open the Bluewater Bay Campus in January and it has had a very strong first year. The impact of Crosspoint's ministries and outreach has made a difference not only in Niceville and Crestview, but also around the world.
