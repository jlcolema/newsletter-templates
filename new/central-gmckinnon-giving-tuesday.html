GIVING TUESDAY

Monday, November 30, 2020



Dear Friends,

We all know about Black Friday and Cyber Monday, but what about "Giving Tuesday"? Giving Tuesday is the Tuesday after Thanksgiving. Churches, charities and other nonprofit organizations make it easy for everyone to give to causes that really make a difference. Crosspoint is participating in Giving Tuesday and encouraging everyone to take a break from spending for a day and consider giving to things that can make an incredible impact on the lives of thousands of people.

Since the financial downturn of 2008, Crosspoint has been encouraging everyone to celebrate Jesus' birthday by giving as much to others through our Advent offerings as we spend on ourselves during Christmas. This year's African Advent offering will go to:

  • Provide pastors in the Congo Bibles in their native language ($20 each)
  • Build much needed churches for the rapidly growing African church (Average $15,000 to 20,000)
  • Buy bicycles for African pastors who have no other means of transportation ($125 each)
  • Train African pastors so they can be even more effective in expanding the Kingdom ($300 per pastor)

You can give to African Advent through "Giving Tuesday" HERE. Everything that is given will go to others. NONE of what you give goes to Crosspoint, it is all used to advance God's Kingdom abroad.

Happy Giving!
