March 22, 2021

Yesterday, we concluded our This is What We Do message series. We saw that living generously is what we do as Christ Followers! At times that means we give spontaneously when we see a need and God nudges us to meet that need. We experience incredible joy when we are used by God in that way. But as Christians, we should also give strategically by planning to be generous. If we don't plan to give systematically, we probably will not be able to be as generous.

However, I believe we discover the greatest joy when we give sacrificially. That's why in our messages yesterday we were challenged to seek to set a new "Personal Giving Record" this year! I'm certain that if you will give radically, generously and sacrificially to God... through a church... whether that's Crosspoint or another Christ-centered church, you will experience unbelievable joy.

I thank everyone who has chosen to give to God through Crosspoint. Because you have responded to God, as a church we can respond and meet needs in our community, build churches in Africa, support ministries that are also making a difference for Christ, and do so much more!

If you were not able to hear yesterday's message, you can listen to it at crosspoint.church/messages/whatwedo/. After you listen to the message, I encourage you to sincerely ask God what he would have you give and then respond obediently. I would appreciate it if you would share that response with us by completing a Giving Response Card that can be found at crosspoint.church/forms/givingresponse/.

Living generously is what we do!
