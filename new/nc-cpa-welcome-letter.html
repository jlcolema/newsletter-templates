Tuition

As stated in the handbook, tuition is an annual fee divided into equal monthly payments. These monthly payments are due on the 1st day of each month starting July 1. Automatic debit payments will be drafted on the 1st working day of each month. Late fees will be assessed after the 10th. If any payment is returned, a $30 return fee will be assessed.

Unexpected Closures & Delayed Start Times

We will send emails and texts concerning unexpected closures or delayed start times whenever possible. We will also update the web page and/or Facebook as we can so you can plan accordingly for upcoming closures/delayed start times.
