FROM RUREL AUSLEY

February 1, 2021

Dear ##LastName## Family,

Wow! What an incredible year 2020 was. If we had been sitting around last year at this time, talking about what we thought 2020 was going to look like, it wouldn't have looked like this. 2020 started out so well, but that changed in March. As a result, this has been a year like no other.

However, our God is an awesome God! He will be redeeming this for years. In the midst of all that has happened, God has used Crosspoint in some really significant ways.

  • We launched the Freeport Campus January 26, 2020.
  • We started our Online Campus before the COVID-19 pandemic began!
  • We supported more than 600 pastors and their families in the Congo and Liberia during the shutdown.
  • We gave Foley First United Methodist Church a truck and paid the first 6 months salary of a part time hurricane coordinator.
  • We saw 88 people accept Christ in the middle of shutdowns and decreased in-person attendance.

We also continued to touch the lives of many people. Bree is just one of them. She says,

"If it wasn't for the church surrounding me after a divorce, the death of my mom, my daughter, and my brother, I don't know where I would be."

She shared a powerful Christmas video testimony of how, when she seemed to have lost everything in 2020, God's love and grace met her every need and she is closer to God than ever before. You can see her video HERE.

I want to thank you for your giving to the Lord through Crosspoint in 2020. Your financial giving enabled us to be on the cutting edge of ministry when the pandemic hit. Immediately, we began pouring into our communities and making a positive impact throughout the pandemic.

In a year of uncertainty, you were able to maintain your level of giving! It means a lot to the Lord and to us that you wanted to make sure that Crosspoint's ministry outreach maintained its momentum.

As thankful as we are for your generosity, we are even more thankful for the gratitude that it represents in your heart for the Lord. In the words of the Apostle Paul from Philippians 4:17, "Not that I desire your gifts; what I desire is that more be credited to your account." Think about that, more being credited to your account. Even as our church finances are strengthened by your gifts, you are the greater beneficiary! Your grateful, cheerful, giving to God changes you!

As we move from 2020 into 2021, we are praying for a year of increasing impact for Crosspoint, that Jesus Christ would be exalted, and He would draw men and women to Himself like never before. I would love for us to be able to say at the end of 2021, that by God's grace, mercy, and favor, the impact through our church has been bigger than we could have ever dreamed.

With your generosity, YOU get to be a vital part of that.

God bless you,


Rurel Ausley, Lead Pastor
