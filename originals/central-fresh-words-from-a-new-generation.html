FRESH WORDS FROM A NEW GENERATION

June 10, 2019

ATTN: Attention 15 to 25 year-old Men and Women!

Let God speak through you! If you're between 15 and 25 and have ever felt like you had a message that God wanted you to spread through preaching, then the "Fresh Words from a New Generation" event is for you. It will be held on Wednesday, July 24 at 6:00 p.m. in the Crosspoint, Niceville Sanctuary.

To participate in the event you must:

  • sign up HERE,
  • attend a Zoom video conference on Monday, June 17,
  • work on a sermon with an assigned one-on-one mentor, and
  • be selected by all the mentors.

We will send you more details on the Zoom video conference once you sign up. If you cannot make the June 17 Zoom meeting, let me know and I will plan an alternate time for you.

We will invite everyone who is a part of Crosspoint to the Wednesday, July 24 event to experience firsthand how God is working through our young leaders.
