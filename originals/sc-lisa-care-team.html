CROSSPOINT SERVES: CARE TEAM

Dear Care Team Member,

Thank you so much for signing up to help with our visitation to the ill. We call this our Care Team. We are thankful that you are willing to serve God and our church family in this important way. I have added you to our Care Team group email.

Periodically, I send out a communique as to who is ill, having surgery, needs encouragement (like a text message or a card), etc. I invite you to respond as you are able. Sometimes, I call on specific people to respond to someone if I think that would be a particularly good fit.

Do let me know when you make a visit and report any new information back to me.

I am attaching a training document which will be helpful in making calls to hospitals or care centers.

If you would like to go with someone else the first time or two, I can set that up! Just let me know. And if you have any questions, feel free to ask.

If I have included you on this list in error, please don't hesitate to let me know.

Thank you for serving!
