NATIONAL DAY OF PRAYER

May 4, 2021

Dear Friends,

Thank you for helping us pray for our nation! Christians across America will be involved in focused prayer and we want to join them! Use this prayer guide and let's pray on Thursday, May 6, 2021.

2021 NATIONAL DAY OF PRAYER - LOVE, LIFE, and LIBERTY:

2 Corinthians 3:17 "Now the Lord is the Spirit and where the Spirit of the Lord is there is freedom."

PRAY FOR AMERICA - 7 CENTERS OF NATIONAL INFLUENCE

  • Government - Pray for Federal, State and Local Officials, Fire and Police Chiefs - 2 Samuel 23:3 "Pray for just leaders who rule in the fear of God." And, Psalm 2:10
  • Military - Pray for our men and women in all branches of armed forces. 2 Chronicles 32:6-8 "He appointed military officers... and encouraged them with these words... with us is the Lord our God to help us and fight our battles."
  • Media - Pray for truth, honesty and integrity in all media sectors. Ephesians 4:25 "Therefore, laying aside falsehood, speak truth each one of you with his neighbor, for we are members of one another."
  • Business - Pray for wisdom, ability, and expertise for Christian Workers. "I have filled him with the Spirit of God, giving him great wisdom, ability, and expertise... he is a master craftsman, expert in working..." Exodus 31:3-4
  • Education - Pray for God's presence and protection over all students and those who teach and administrate in America's classrooms and in homeschool settings. "If you call out for insight and cry aloud for understanding... then you will understand the fear of the Lord and find the knowledge of God." Proverbs 3-6
  • Church - Pray the Church in America will find new zeal to be strong in our faith and be fruitful in sharing the healing love of Jesus Christ with the hurting. "Therefore, go make disciples of all nations baptizing them in the name of the Father and the Son and the Holy Spirit." Matthew 28:18
  • Family - Pray for God's blessings of love and unity among parents and children. Psalm 127:4-5 "Like arrows in the hand of a warrior, so are children born in one's youth. Blessed is the man whose quiver is full of them." Numbers 6:24-26 "May the Lord bless you and keep you... be gracious to you and grant you His peace."

See you Sunday,
